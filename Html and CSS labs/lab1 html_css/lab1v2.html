Ron Burgundy

I don’t know how to put this, but I’m kind of a big deal. People know me. I’m very important. I have many leather-bound books, and my apartment smells of rich mahogany.

The man, the legend....

The story of this legendary anchorman begins in San Diego California in the 1970's where Mr. Burgundy led the highest rated news team in the region. His life story which became an uplifiting film and insperation to all, details the rise, fall and rise again of this icon.
During the span of his epic career he appeared on the evening news in these cities:

  • San Diego,CA
  • Los Angeles,CA
  • New York City, NY
  • Jacksonville, FL
